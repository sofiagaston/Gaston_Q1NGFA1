Percy Jackson and the Olympians by Rick Riordan

  • A 5 book fiction series that follows teenage personalities on their intense adventures, which may or may not involve certain Greek Gods.
  • The first book, Percy Jackson and the Olympians: The Lightning Thief, was published on JUne 28, 2005.
  • They had already published a movie adaptation of the first 2 books, but the book's author wasn't satisfied with it. So he signed with Disney Plus to create a Percy Jackson series adaptation, which will come out this December 20th.
